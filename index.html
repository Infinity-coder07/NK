About Us

Nandankanana is a place of joy and happiness. It is a place where you can find peace and tranquility. The beauty of nature is at its best here. The animals and birds are a sight to behold. The greenery and the fresh air make it a perfect getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life. Nandankanana is a place where you can relax and rejuvenate. It is a place where you can connect with nature and find inner peace. The serene environment and the soothing sounds of nature make it a perfect place to unwind and recharge your batteries. Nandankanana is a place where you can create memories that will last a lifetime.
